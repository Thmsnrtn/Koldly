Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

1. Introduction

Koldly ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process personal information in connection with our website and services (the "Service").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.
  • Payment Information: We collect billing address and payment method information through Stripe. We do not directly store full credit card details.
  • Campaign Data: Information about your campaigns, including prospect lists, email templates, and outreach sequences.
  • Communication: Messages you send us through support or contact forms.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, time spent on pages, clicks, and actions taken within the Service.
  • Device Information: Device type, browser type, IP address, and operating system.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to remember your preferences and enhance your experience.
  • Analytics: Third-party analytics tools help us understand how users interact with our Service.

2.3 Third-Party Data

  • OAuth Providers: If you sign in with Google, we receive your public profile information.
  • Prospect Data: Information about prospects you upload or that we discover through public research.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the Service
  • To process payments and send billing information
  • To send transactional emails (password resets, verification, receipts)
  • To send promotional communications (with your consent)
  • To detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve user experience
  • To generate AI-powered prospect research and email content

4. AI Usage Disclosure

Koldly uses artificial intelligence and machine learning to:

  • Generate personalized email content
  • Research and enrich prospect information
  • Analyze campaign performance
  • Identify ideal customer profiles

By using Koldly, you consent to the processing of your data through AI systems. We do not use your campaign data to train models that identify individual users.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third parties who help operate our Service (hosting, analytics, payment processing)
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business Transfers: In the event of acquisition or merger, your information may be transferred
  • Consent: With your explicit permission

6. CAN-SPAM Compliance

Koldly helps you comply with the CAN-SPAM Act and other anti-spam regulations. You agree to:

  • Only use Koldly to send emails to recipients who have opted in or have a legitimate business relationship with you
  • Include accurate sender information and a valid physical address in all emails
  • Respect unsubscribe requests and honor opt-out preferences
  • Honor all applicable laws regarding commercial email communication

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption, firewalls, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. You acknowledge that security risks cannot be entirely eliminated.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, portable format
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of marketing communications

10. GDPR & International Compliance

If you are located in the European Union or other jurisdictions with similar data protection laws, Koldly complies with applicable regulations including GDPR. Our legal basis for processing your information includes your consent, contract performance, and legitimate business interests.

11. Third-Party Links

Koldly may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

12. Children's Privacy

Koldly is not intended for individuals under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will take steps to delete the information and terminate the child's account.

13. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
