Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! All plans are month-to-month. Cancel anytime from your dashboard with no penalties or long-term contracts.
What happens if I exceed my prospect limit?
You can upgrade to the next tier anytime. We'll never shut off your campaigns mid-month — you'll just need to upgrade before creating new ones.
Do you offer a free trial?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee. Try any plan risk-free, and if you're not satisfied, we'll refund you in full.
Can I switch plans later?
Absolutely! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the difference.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) through Stripe. Your payment info is securely processed and never stored on our servers.
